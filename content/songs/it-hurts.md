# 🎵 IT HURTS! — San Holo

> *A glitchy, guitar-soaked meditation on the paradox that pain is proof you're alive — it's only real when it hurts*

---

## 📀 About the Song

- **Released:** 2021
- **Album:** bb u ok?
- **Genre:** Indie Electronic, Future Bass, Lo-fi
- **In one line:** A man embraces the idea that pain — in love and in life — is what makes everything feel real.

---

## 🎭 Themes & Emotions

"IT HURTS!" takes a feeling most people try to avoid and reframes it as something essential. Pain, San Holo argues, isn't the opposite of living well. It's proof that you are living. The central philosophy — "it's only real when it hurts" — flips the conventional wisdom. We spend our lives trying to minimise pain, but this song says: pay attention to it. The pain means something. It means you care. It means you're not numb. It means you're still here.

San Holo — the Dutch producer and musician born Sander van Dijck — wrote the song about love but has said it applies to life in general. That openness is deliberate. The pain of a breakup, the pain of creative struggle, the pain of growing up, the pain of simply being alive in a confusing world — it all qualifies. The song doesn't specify which kind of hurt. It just acknowledges that hurt is happening, and that's okay.

The production is quintessentially San Holo: warm, warbly synths that sound slightly broken, guitar lines that bend and shimmer, and vocals that sit somewhere between singing and speaking. Everything sounds slightly imperfect, like a beautiful photograph with light leaks. That deliberate imperfection is the sonic equivalent of the song's message — the cracks and flaws are where the feeling lives.

There's also a strange comfort in the song's energy. Despite its title, "IT HURTS!" doesn't sound depressed. It sounds alive. The beat moves forward, the guitar shimmers, the voice carries a quiet confidence. It's not a cry for help. It's a statement of fact delivered with acceptance: yes, this hurts. And that's exactly how it should be.

## 📖 Lyrics: Key Lines & What They Mean

### "It's only real when it hurts"

**What it means:** Authentic experience — real love, real growth, real living — involves pain. If it doesn't hurt at all, it might not be real.

**Why it matters:** This is the song's central thesis. It reframes pain not as a problem to solve but as evidence of genuine engagement with life.

---

### "Sometimes feeling pain is better than feeling nothing at all"

**What it means:** Numbness — emotional flatness, the absence of feeling — is worse than pain. At least pain confirms you're alive and capable of caring.

**Why it matters:** This speaks directly to anyone who has experienced emotional shutdown. It validates the preference for feeling, even when feeling means hurting.

---

### "It hurts"

**What it means:** A simple, direct acknowledgment. No explanation, no justification. Just: this hurts.

**Why it matters:** Sometimes the most powerful thing you can say is the most obvious. Naming pain is the first step toward processing it.

## 🌍 Cultural & Historical Context

San Holo is the project of Sander van Dijck, a Dutch musician and producer from Zoetermeer, Netherlands, born in 1990. He emerged from the electronic music scene but always stood apart from it — more interested in guitars and vocals than in drops and builds. His debut album *Album1* (2018) blended future bass with indie rock, and by the time *bb u ok?* arrived in 2021, he had fully established himself as an artist who uses electronic production as a vehicle for emotional songwriting.

"IT HURTS!" was released during the pandemic era, a time when millions of people were reckoning with isolation, anxiety, and the strange numbness that comes from living through a crisis that never seems to end. The song's message — that pain is better than numbness, that hurt means you're still feeling something — resonated powerfully with listeners who were struggling to feel anything at all.

The album title *bb u ok?* — text-speak for "baby, are you okay?" — captures San Holo's artistic ethos: genuine emotional concern expressed in the language of the internet generation. It's not ironic. It's sincere. And that sincerity, delivered through glitchy, imperfect production, has made San Holo one of the most emotionally authentic voices in electronic music.

- The album *bb u ok?* (2021) was written and released during the pandemic, making its themes of emotional pain and numbness particularly resonant.

## 🧑‍🎤 About the Artist

San Holo is the project of Sander van Dijck, a Dutch musician, producer, and guitarist born in 1990 in Zoetermeer, Netherlands. Blending electronic production with indie rock guitar and emotionally vulnerable vocals, he's carved a unique space between EDM festivals and bedroom headphone listening. His albums *Album1* (2018) and *bb u ok?* (2021) prove that electronic music can be deeply, authentically emotional.

## 💬 Why This Song Is Worth Your Time

"IT HURTS!" teaches you one of the most important emotional concepts in English: the relationship between pain and authenticity. Phrases like "it's only real when," "feeling nothing at all," and the simple declaration "it hurts" are essential for honest conversations about emotion in English. But the song's deeper gift is philosophical: it gives you permission to feel bad. In a culture that constantly tells you to be positive and optimise for happiness, this song says: the hurt is real, and real is valuable.
